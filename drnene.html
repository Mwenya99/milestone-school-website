School Profile 2023 - 2024

Milestone School is a mixed Nursery and Primary school located in Luangwa. The school is built on the vision of creating a strong and sustainable center of learning that offers affordable and high quality education to the community. The school opened in January 2008 and now has more than thirty-five members of staff. The teaching staff is highly qualified and experienced.

Programs & Activities

Strong literacy and numeracy programs, science and student wellbeing. Extra-curriculars include sports, art, media, STEAM, soccer, volleyball, netball, and ICT.

Enrolment & Growth

Started in 2008 with six nursery children. Now offers Nursery to Primary Seven and supports 400+ learners. A second campus is expected by December 2024.

Director Profile

Miranda Nene: The Mind Behind Milestone School

Miranda Mutinta Nene is an accomplished educator with more than a decade of experience. She holds a Bachelor of Education and a Master of Education from Mulungushi University. Over sixteen years, she has managed Milestone School and worked with several organizations, including serving as a teacher with the Ministry of Education.

Her skills include team leadership, attention to detail, problem-solving, and strong communication. She has guided the school in community efforts and bursary programs, and served in humanitarian activities locally.
