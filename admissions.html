Admissions

Our Process

  1. Inquiry: Contact our administration office to express interest and ask any preliminary questions. We're happy to help!
  2. Application: Download and complete the application form. Submit it to our office along with the required documents.
  3. Assessment: Prospective students will be invited for a friendly, age-appropriate assessment to understand their learning level.
  4. Offer of Place: Successful applicants will receive an official offer of admission from the school.
  5. Important Dates and Deadlines: Successful applicants will receive an official offer of admission from the school.

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of the child's birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Copy of immunization records
